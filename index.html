Bengaluru Bulls

Bengaluru Bulls is a professional kabaddi team based in Bengaluru that plays in the Pro Kabaddi League. The team were champions in Season 6 and are led by Saurabh Nandal and coached by Randhir Singh. The team is owned by Kosmik Global Media.

History and Records

  • Bengaluru Bulls are one of the most consistent performers in the history of the Pro Kabaddi League. They have reached the playoffs in four out of seven seasons so far.
  • Bengaluru Bulls won the trophy for the first time by defeating the Gujarat FortuneGiants in the 2018â€“19 season. They were also the runner-up to U Mumba in 2015 and reached the semifinals in the inaugural 2014 season.
  • Pawan Kumar Sehrawat is the star raider of the team and the league. He has scored 673 raid points in his PKL career, the third-highest among all raiders. He was also the best raider in Season 6 and 7, scoring 271 and 346 raid points respectively.
  • Mahender Singh is the best defender of the team and one of the best in the league. He has scored 181 tackle points in his PKL career, the 12th-highest among all defenders. He was also the best defender of the team in Season 5, 6 and 7, scoring 57, 63 and 61 tackle points respectively.
  • Manjeet Chhillar is the most successful all-rounder of the team and the league. He has scored 229 total points in his PKL career, the highest among all all-rounders. He was also the captain and the best player of the team in Season 1 and 2, scoring 122 and 107 total points respectively.
